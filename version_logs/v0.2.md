# v0.2 Version Log

Date: 2026-06-18

## Goal

Add first-class bilingual support for Chinese patients who know no English.

Chinese support is implemented independently rather than through browser/page translation.

## User-Facing Changes

- Chinese (`zh-CN`) is now the default interface language.
- Added language selector for:
  - Chinese
  - English
- Added Chinese UI labels, page title, state labels, buttons, placeholders, and report placeholder.
- Added Chinese call-style assistant prompts.
- Added Chinese speech recognition language setting (`zh-CN`) when browser speech recognition is available.
- Added Chinese text-to-speech voice preference when the browser/OS exposes Chinese voices.
- Added Chinese doctor report output.

Chinese opening line:

```text
您好，我是北京大学医学部医院的研究人员。
想做一个简短的关节疼痛随访。
先确认一下，您的姓名、手机号和年龄是多少？
```

## Chinese Validation

Added Chinese-aware validation for:

- Chinese pain score inputs such as `五分`
- Chinese invalid answers such as `咖啡`
- Chinese ambiguous severe pain such as `疼死了`
- Chinese functional impact such as `上下楼有点困难`
- Chinese yes/no answers such as `有`, `没有`, `没`, `无`
- Chinese uncertainty such as `不知道`, `不确定`
- Chinese pain comparison such as `重了`, `轻了`, `差不多`

Existing contradiction logic remains active in Chinese mode.

## Known Limitations

- Browser speech recognition support for `zh-CN` depends on the browser and OS.
- Browser Chinese text-to-speech voice quality depends on installed/exposed voices.
- Chinese parsing is rule-based and does not cover all dialectal or colloquial phrasing.
- Reports are either Chinese or English based on session language; a fully bilingual side-by-side doctor report is not yet implemented.
- Local Qwen integration remains optional and off by default.
- No production privacy, consent, encryption, audit logging, or clinician dashboard is implemented.
